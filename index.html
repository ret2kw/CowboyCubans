The Story

Connor grew up salt-crusted and sun-soaked in Key West, surrounded by Cuban caf√©s and coladas on every corner. Starting young in the industry his first experience was working mom's food cart before he could see over the counter.

Then his parents got "real jobs" and dragged him to Austin. Trading turquoise waters for Texas heat. But the kitchen never left him. From that first cart to the family restaurant to working at Michelin star kitchens across the world, he's been chasing the perfect bite his whole life.

"Every sandwich is a love letter to Key West, slow roasted mojo pork, pressed cuban bread that crunches like I remember, flavors that taste like home even 1,500 miles away."
